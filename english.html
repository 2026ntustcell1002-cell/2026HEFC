HEFC 2026

The 2026 National Hydrogen Energy and Fuel Cell Conference will be held on October 2-3, 2026 at the International Building, National Taiwan University of Science and Technology.

Date
October 2-3, 2026
Venue
International Building, NTUST

About the Conference

Hydrogen energy and fuel cell technologies are key components of the global pathway toward net-zero carbon emissions. HEFC 2026 aims to connect frontier academic research with industrial implementation, focusing on technical breakthroughs, academia-industry collaboration, and the transition toward sustainable energy systems.

Conference Goals

Exchange

Technical Exchange

Promote academic and applied exchange in hydrogen energy and fuel cell technologies.

Industry

Academia-Industry Matching

Connect research capabilities with industrial technology needs.

Talent

Talent Development

Encourage young researchers and students to engage with green energy topics.

Policy

Policy Dialogue

Provide a dialogue platform for government, academia, and industry.

Program Overview

Day 1

Industry Forum｜October 2, 2026

Registration, forum opening, invited talks, coffee breaks, lunch, panel discussion, and forum dinner. Main sessions will take place in IB-101.

Day 2

Academic Conference｜October 3, 2026

Opening ceremony, keynote talks, student paper competition, oral sessions, poster presentations, lunch, and conference banquet.

Topics

Hydrogen Energy

Hydrogen production, purification, storage, water electrolysis, and power-to-gas technologies.

Fuel Cells

Low-temperature and high-temperature fuel cell materials and systems.

Additional topic details are under preparation.
