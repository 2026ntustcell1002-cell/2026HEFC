Transportation

MRT

Gongguan Station Exit 2

From Exit 2, walk along Keelung Road toward Gongguan Circle for about 8-10 minutes to reach the NTUST main gate.

Transfer

Liuzhangli / Jingan Station

From Liuzhangli Station, transfer to bus 1, 207, 672, or Keelung Road Main Line. From Jingan Station, transfer to bus 908 or Green 10.

Bus

NTUST Stop

Available routes include 1, 207, 254, 275, 650, 672, 673, 905, 906, 909, Keelung Road Main Line, and Dunhua Main Line.
