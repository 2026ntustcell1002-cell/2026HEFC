FAQ

Registration and Payment

What happens after completing the registration form?

After completing the online registration form, the system will send a confirmation email automatically.

When can I receive the official receipt?

After payment is completed, official receipts will be issued by the conference and collected at on-site check-in.

Paper Submission

What is the abstract length limit?

The abstract length limit is within 500 Chinese characters.

Is a specific format required?

Yes. Please use the designated Word template when it is officially released.

What if the presenting author cannot attend?

A co-author may present on behalf of the author, but the conference should be notified in advance.
