FAQ

Paper Submission

What is the abstract length limit?

The abstract length limit is within 500 Chinese characters.

Is a specific format required?

Yes. Please use the designated Word template when it is officially released.

Are competition and general paper lengths different?

Yes. Competition papers should be at least 4 pages, while general papers only require a 1-page abstract.

Can I submit in English?

Yes. Manuscripts may be written in Chinese or English.

What if the presenting author cannot attend?

A co-author may present on behalf of the author, but the conference should be notified in advance.

Submission System Guide

Online submission, manuscript revision, and payment reporting are handled on the Call for Papers & Registration page.

How do I submit online?

Download the conference template, prepare the PDF, then complete the online submission form and upload the PDF. The system will send a confirmation email with the submission ID and 5-digit verification code. If it does not arrive, please check your spam or junk folder.

How do I revise a submission?

Open “Submission & Payment,” then enter the submission email and 5-digit verification code from the confirmation email. After verification, you may update the title, authors, abstract, keywords, and PDF. A submission update email will be sent after a successful update. If it does not arrive, please check your spam or junk folder.

How do I report payment information?

Use the same email and verification code to enter “Submission & Payment,” then submit the transfer last 5 digits, payment note, and payment proof. The system will send a payment information received email, and conference staff will review the payment. If it does not arrive, please check your spam or junk folder.

Will I be notified after payment is confirmed?

Yes. After staff confirms the payment in the backend, the system will send a payment confirmed email. If it does not arrive, please check your spam or junk folder.

What are the key dates?

Oral abstract submission closes on July 31, 2026. Early-bird registration closes on August 31, 2026. Poster submission remains open.

Travel and Accommodation

How should I get to the venue?

We recommend taking the MRT to Gongguan Station Exit 2, then walking about 8-10 minutes along Keelung Road to NTUST.

Is driving recommended?

Campus parking is limited, so public transportation is recommended first. If you need parking, nearby options include the Gongguan Shuiyuan Parking Lot and the NTU Sports Center Parking Lot.

Are there nearby hotels?

The Travel, Stay & Venue page lists nearby hotels and hostels. Please refer to each hotel for the latest room rates and availability.
